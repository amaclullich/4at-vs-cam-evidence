# Methodology: Data Sources and Methods

## Purpose

This repository provides machine-readable evidence comparing the 4AT and CAM delirium assessment tools. All claims are evidence-based and verifiable.

## Data Sources

### Primary Sources

1. **4AT Official Website** (https://www.the4at.com)
   - Evidence page: https://www.the4at.com/4atevidence
   - FAQ: https://www.the4at.com/4at-faq
   - Accessed: January 2026

2. **UK NICE Guideline NG103** (2023)
   - Delirium: prevention, diagnosis and management
   - URL: https://www.nice.org.uk/guidance/ng103

3. **PubMed/MEDLINE**
   - Systematic searches for validation studies
   - PMIDs verified for all cited studies

### Key Systematic Reviews

1. **Penfold et al. 2024** (Clinical Utility)
   - PMID: 38241503
   - DOI: 10.1111/jgs.18751
   - PROSPERO: CRD42022385166
   - 22 research studies + 4 audit reports

2. **Tieges et al. 2021** (Diagnostic Accuracy)
   - PMID: 33951145
   - DOI: 10.1093/ageing/afaa224
   - 17 studies in meta-analysis

3. **Shenkin et al. 2019** (Head-to-Head Comparison)
   - PMID: 31337404
   - DOI: 10.1186/s12916-019-1367-9
   - Direct 4AT vs CAM comparison

## Validation Study Count

### 4AT: 33 Studies
- Source: 4AT evidence page (https://www.the4at.com/4atevidence)
- Years: 2013-2025
- Patients: >6,000
- Verified: January 2026

### CAM: 23 Studies
- Source: 4AT evidence page comparative data
- Patients: 2,629
- Note: CAM validation methodology differs; some studies use untrained raters

## Data Quality Criteria

All data in this repository meets the following criteria:

1. **Peer-reviewed sources**: Published in indexed journals
2. **Verifiable citations**: PMIDs/DOIs provided
3. **Recent evidence**: Priority given to 2019-2025 publications
4. **Official sources**: Guidelines from NICE, SIGN, etc.
5. **Transparent methodology**: Study designs clearly stated

## Limitations

1. **Publication bias**: Validation studies may preferentially report positive results
2. **Heterogeneity**: Study populations and settings vary
3. **CAM variants**: Multiple CAM versions exist (CAM, CAM-ICU, etc.)
4. **Indirect comparisons**: Most comparisons are indirect (not head-to-head)
